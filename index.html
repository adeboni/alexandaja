Stanford University

450 Serra Mall · Stanford, CA 94305

Stanford University is a must-see campus for those who have never been there. Take a stroll around the quad, visit the Cantor Arts Center Museum (which offers free admission!), grab a coffee at the Coupa Cafe stand near the library or a frozen yogurt at Fraiche Yogurt. It's the world's second largest college campus, so you can spend a lot of time exploring.
